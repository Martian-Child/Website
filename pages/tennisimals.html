Tennisimals

About

Tennisimals is a simple game I made for a course I took at Western Washington University, Virtual Worlds. Its inspired by Nintendos Wii Sports Tennis and Sega Super Starts Tennis. It supports gamepad and mouse/keyboard and features couch coop, one level, a main menu, pause menu and some basic tennis gameplay with scores, out of bounds, fouls, etc. In the future I would like to add more levels, a better looking UI, a functional options menu, and character selection.
